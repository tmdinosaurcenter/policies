# Timekeeping

All non-exempt employees are required to accurately record all hours worked using TMDC's designated timekeeping system. You are responsible for clocking in at the start of your shift and clocking out at the end.

Do not clock in early or remain clocked in after your shift without supervisor authorization. Do not ask another employee to clock in or out on your behalf, and do not clock in or out for another employee. Falsification of time records is grounds for immediate termination.

If you identify an error in your time record, notify your supervisor promptly so it can be corrected before the pay period closes.
