# Probationary Period

All new employees will have an initial probationary period of six (6) months beginning on their first day of work. The purpose of this period is to allow evaluation of performance, punctuality, work ethic, attitude, and dependability.

During the probationary period, employment may be terminated by either party at any time, with or without cause and with or without notice.

Because most TMDC seasonal positions have terms of three to four months, many seasonal employees will work their entire term within the probationary period.

Completion of the probationary period does not guarantee continued employment or renewal for any future season.
