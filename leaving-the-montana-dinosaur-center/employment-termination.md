# Employment Termination

**Voluntary Resignation**

If you choose to leave TMDC, please provide a minimum of two weeks' written notice to your supervisor, indicating your last day of work. Final wages following voluntary resignation will be paid by the next regular payday after your last day of work, as required by Montana law (MCA §39-3-205).

On or before your last day, the Executive Director or your supervisor will schedule an exit interview to gather feedback about your experience.

**Involuntary Termination**

For employees who have completed the six-month probationary period, TMDC may terminate employment only for good cause. For employees within the probationary period, employment may be terminated at any time, with or without cause.

Final wages following involuntary termination will be paid as soon as practicable, and no later than the next regular payday, as required by Montana law.

**Accommodation upon Separation**

If you are occupying a TMDC staff accommodation trailer, your license to use that accommodation terminates automatically upon your last day of employment. You must vacate and remove all personal belongings within 48 hours of your separation date.
