# Workers' Compensation

TMDC carries workers' compensation insurance. All employees are covered from their first day of work.

If you are injured on the job, you must report the injury to your supervisor immediately. Failure to timely report a workplace injury may affect your eligibility for benefits.

Workers' compensation covers medical expenses and, in some cases, wage replacement for injuries that arise out of and in the course of employment. It does not cover injuries that occur outside of work duties.
